# Display

Energy flow visualization subsystems for the BEV system model. These display models are referenced by the vehicle templates to show real-time power and energy flow during simulation.

---

## Display Models

| Model | Used With |
|-------|-----------|
| EnergyElectric | VehicleElectric, VehicleElecAux |
| EnergyElectroThermal | VehicleElectroThermal, VehicleElectroThermalLowTemp |

The electrical display shows battery, motor, and charger power flow. The electro-thermal display adds thermal loop energy flow (coolant, chiller, heater, radiator).
